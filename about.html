About

Washcoll.net is a co-operative project to house services that Washington College cannot or will not host. We host an IRC bouncer, a wiki and web space for projects. We strive to provide these services to the Washington College community regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, employment status, class year, or major. If you would like to have a project hosted at Washcoll.net or a bouncer account, contact a sysop.

Privacy Policy

 Private information including, but not limited to, IP addresses, user agents, MAC addresses, real names, email addresses, cookies used, and geolocation will only be shared with individuals in possession of a valid search warrant, subpena, writ, or other document issued by a court set up by the federal government of the United States of America, the Kingdom of the Netherlands, or the State of Maryland. Writs and other documents issued by the courts of other nations, states, provinces, municipalities, boards, or bodies will not be accepted.
